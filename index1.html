Informatique Recipe

Introduction

This delicious desert can be served with drink or kool-aid. This recipe produces 40 juice.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 200ml water
  • 50g sugar

Steps

  1. Put it in the refrigerator;
  2. Reduce sugar to powder
  3. Add water and the egg white
  4. form small cups of 2cm
  5. leave it in this freezer for 20 min
